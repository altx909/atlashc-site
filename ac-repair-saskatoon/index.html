Signs Your AC Needs Repair

Don't wait until your system fails completely. These are the most common warning signs Saskatoon homeowners call us about:

Not Cooling Properly

Blowing warm or lukewarm air — could be a refrigerant issue, dirty coil, or failing compressor.

Weak Airflow

Poor airflow through vents often points to a blower motor issue, clogged filter, or duct problem.

Unusual Noises

Banging, rattling, squealing, or clicking sounds are never normal — something is wrong inside the unit.

Water or Ice Buildup

Pooling water or ice on your unit signals a refrigerant leak or airflow restriction that needs attention now.

Short Cycling

AC turning on and off constantly wastes energy and puts serious stress on your compressor.

Spiking Hydro Bills

A sudden jump in your electricity bill often means your AC is working too hard due to a hidden issue.
