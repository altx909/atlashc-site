Signs Your Furnace Needs Repair

Don't wait until your heat goes out completely. These are the warning signs Saskatoon homeowners most commonly miss:

Insufficient Heat

Runs constantly but never warms your home to the set temperature.

Short Cycling

Furnace turns on and off repeatedly in short bursts.

Strange Noises

Banging, squealing, or rattling coming from your furnace.

Yellow Pilot Light

Should be blue. Yellow can indicate a carbon monoxide issue.

High Energy Bills

Sudden spike in gas or electric costs without a change in usage.

Won't Turn On

Thermostat is set correctly but furnace won't start at all.
